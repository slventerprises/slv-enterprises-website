Who We Are

Built on Quality,
Driven by Precision

SLV Enterprises is a Bengaluru-based manufacturer specialising in silver-finish paper plates and their raw materials (laminated blanks). Operating out of the Vijayanagar–Kamakshipalya corridor, we have been a reliable supply partner for distributors, wholesalers, event caterers, and export agents across Karnataka and beyond.

Every plate leaving our facility goes through strict quality checks — from raw material grammage and silver coating uniformity to finished plate strength and stacking precision. We manufacture both the finished paper plates and the raw materials, giving our clients the flexibility to choose what fits their operation.
